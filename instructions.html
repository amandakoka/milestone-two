How to play the World Flags Quiz

  1. Click the "Start Quiz" button on the main page to begin the quiz.
  2. You will be presented with an image of a flag and four answer options.
  3. Select the correct country for the displayed flag by clicking on one of the answer buttons.
  4. Your score will be updated based on your correct and incorrect answers.
  5. A timer will keep track of the time you take to complete the quiz.
  6. You can view your progress on the progress bar at the bottom of the quiz screen.
  7. Click the "Next" button to proceed to the next question.
  8. At the end of the quiz, your total score and time will be displayed.

Good luck, and have fun!
